About Me



I started in touch with guitar when I was F4. I was just randomly joined the music society and the tutor had the heart to teach which I learnt the fundamental skills quickly. After I got high interet in guitar, I played day and night and want to be a better guitar player. I dreamed to be a guitarist.




I have been the chairman of two music society at my secondary school. Also I have experience in performing a band show for several times. I usually have band shows at school at special days like Christmas, New Year, Graduation, etc. And I really enjoy to show off myself on every shows.






My band



This is my band - EMC. EMC means English Music Club. From the left of the photo above, the first one was our teacher Mr. Li. He is very kind and friendly that always booking places for our pratice use. The second one is Ken, he was the bass player. The third one is Doggy, he was the drum player that he always hit the drum hard and very annoying. The forth one is me, the right-guitar player who was responsible to play the solo parts. The last one is Walter, he was the left-guitar player who was responsible for playing the background cover chords.

I made this band with my friends at my secondary school as we all have high interst in music and want to try to form a band. We form other band at F5 and I have learnt guitar about half year. It shocked me that my friends elect me to be the band leader. I am happy to be a leader but also carry a big responsibility as well. I had to communicate with teachers about shows, organised a show and prepared pratices, arrangments to my fellow band members. In addition, our band is under the soicety - English Music Club. Which means that I have to make weekly activities with my society members. Big workload but achieved a lots.

My Guitar

This is my personal electric guitar, also my first guitar as well. Before I bought my guitar, I use acoustic guitar which provided by school. The band of this guitar is Washburn. I bought it in Taipo Parsons Music Limited for around HK$1,500. I loved it as its' neck perfectly fit my hand also it's in red! Feel so rock that looks awesome! It is a 6-string based eletric guitar with a port that can plugin the wire to the speakers. I mostly use a pick to play it. Sometimes I just use my hands to play songs if the songs were in chord-solo. It had many shows with me for 3 years. And It will keep staying with me.
